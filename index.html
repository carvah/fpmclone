Multiplayer client/server demo

Clone (with a few changes) of Gabriel Gambetta's original demo. His series of articles explaining Fast-Paced Multiplayer games is an excellent read.

I made this clone in order to better understand the concepts shown in the original, and to learn about running TypeScript projects.
